The Story of King Louis XI

King Louis XI, known as the Universal Spider, was a king renowned for his complex political maneuvers and efforts to strengthen the central authority of the French monarchy.

Timeline of Key Events

  • 1423: Birth

    Louis XI was born on July 3, 1423, as the son of Charles VII and Marie of Anjou.

  • 1461: Accession to the Throne

    Following the death of his father, Louis XI ascended to the throne and began a reign marked by political intrigue and efforts to consolidate power.

  • 1465: War of the Public Weal

    Louis XI faced rebellion from the nobility, known as the War of the Public Weal, which he managed to suppress, strengthening his position.

  • 1483: Death

    King Louis XI died on August 30, 1483. His reign had a lasting impact on the centralization of the French monarchy.

Legacy

Louis XI is remembered for his shrewd political strategies and efforts to centralize royal power, which had a profound influence on the future of France.
