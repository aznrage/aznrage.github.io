The Story of King Louis XVIII

King Louis XVIII, the brother of Louis XVI, played a crucial role in the restoration of the French monarchy after the fall of Napoleon.

Timeline of Key Events

  • 1755: Birth

    Louis XVIII was born on November 17, 1755, as the younger brother of Louis XVI.

  • 1814: First Restoration

    After Napoleon's first abdication, Louis XVIII was restored to the throne in 1814.

  • 1815: Hundred Days

    Louis XVIII was briefly displaced by Napoleon during the Hundred Days but returned to the throne after Napoleon's final defeat.

  • 1824: Death

    Louis XVIII died on September 16, 1824, and was succeeded by his brother Charles X.

Legacy

Louis XVIII's reign is remembered for the restoration of the Bourbon monarchy and efforts to stabilize France after years of revolutionary and Napoleonic turmoil.
