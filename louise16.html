The Story of King Louis XVI

King Louis XVI, the last monarch of France before the fall of the monarchy during the French Revolution, faced significant challenges during a period of great political and social upheaval.

Timeline of Key Events

  • 1754: Birth

    Louis XVI was born on August 23, 1754, as the son of Louis, Dauphin of France, and Marie-Josèphe of Saxony.

  • 1774: Accession to the Throne

    Following the death of his grandfather, Louis XV, Louis XVI became king of France at the age of 20.

  • 1789: French Revolution Begins

    The French Revolution began, leading to widespread social and political changes, and eventually the downfall of the monarchy.

  • 1793: Execution

    Louis XVI was executed by guillotine on January 21, 1793, marking a significant moment in the French Revolution.

Legacy

Louis XVI's reign is marked by his struggle to address the financial and social issues facing France, culminating in the French Revolution. His legacy is a reflection of the turbulent period that led to the end of the monarchy.
